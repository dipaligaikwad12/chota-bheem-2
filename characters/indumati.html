Rajkumari Indumati

Indumati is the Princess of Dholakpur, the daughter of Indraverma, and the elder sister of Ravi Varma. She is a kindhearted 7-year old girl. She is also a friend of Bheem, Chutki, Raju, Jaggu, Kalia, Dholu and Bholu. She has blue eyes, and reddish-brown hair, and wears a blue and gold lehenga with an orange flower adorning her hair in a low ponytail. While she seldom joins the gang on missions, she is a trusted friend and supports them.
